Hardware Features

One or Two Hands

Test and train only one hand at a time for extra precission or connect another one and use both to find imbalances and save time.

Completely Wireless

Bluetooth connection and an optional battery to ensure the training and testing area remains as clutter free as possible.

Easy Instalation

Thanks to using off the shelf (rust proof and way over specced) components, all you need for installation is a drill and a spanner.

Low Price

Lower cost than other devices but no compromises! Having access to the newest technology shouldn't be restricted by price.

Accurate & Precise

Able to measure grams, take a sample every 12.5 miliseconds and with a calibration proccess that takes less than a minute, all data will be as accurate as possible

Endless Options

You can attach any hangboard, edge, hold or even a replica of outdoor boulders or routes! The only limit is your imagination. Find out more
